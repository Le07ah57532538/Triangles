TYPES OF TRIANGLES BASED ON LENGTH

EQUILATERAL TRIANGLES

Equilateral triangles are triangles with both sides having equal length.

ISOSCELES TRIANGLES

Isosceles triangles are triangles with only opposite sides having similar length.

SCALENE TRIANGLES

Scalene triangles are triangles with sides of different length.
